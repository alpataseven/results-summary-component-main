- Light slate blue (background): hsl(252, 100%, 67%)
- Light royal blue (background): hsl(241, 81%, 54%)

- Violet blue (circle): hsla(256, 72%, 46%, 1)
- Persian blue (circle): hsla(241, 72%, 46%, 0)
